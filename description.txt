It is a polite extension that pauses the video when you are not watching it.
Features:
- Automatically pauses YouTube videos when you switch tabs, minimize the window, or lock your screen.
- Automatically resumes playback when you return to the YouTube tab.
- Enforces a 1.5x playback speed to save you time.
- Handy "Active/Disabled" toggle in the popup to pause the extension's functionality without uninstalling.
- "Made by Harsh Sukhwal" branding.
